  • Login / Connect Wallet
    Authentication

    On the Login page You can either authenticate using the connect wallet (Default) or by email/password.
    Once you've approved a connection request from the Our application, We can send transaction requests to your Bitcoin/crypto Wallet, which you must also manually approve in the Wallet to submit transaction to the network for confirmation. Please remember we are only using ether(ETH). Future releases will also use BEP20 tokens. To have a hassle-free experience, we recommend using MetaMask or TrustWallet.

  • Investment Page
    Investment

    Investors can stake as many times as they want. There is no limit. Investments are locked for the plan duration period and withdrawals can only be made on or after the last day of investment returns You can see our available plans on the plans section of your dashboard

  • Withdrawal Page
    Withdrawal

    There is no reinvest/compound option in the contract, but because of this, users have complete control over their gains. Users with high investment value can have high returns in less than a day. Because We offers high APR yields, an example of a whale investing 20 ETH can accumulate 24 ETH in 24 hours (in Plan 4 with 8% daily). To combat this exploit, we have added a few simple tweaks to the contract.

    WITHDRAWAL RATE LIMITS

    Withdrawal Cooldown: 24 Hours

    Maximum per Withdraw: 10 ETH
